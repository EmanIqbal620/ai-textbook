# Hardware Requirements

This section outlines the hardware requirements for developing and running the examples in this textbook.

## Minimum Specifications

For basic functionality and simulation:

- **CPU**: Intel i7 or AMD Ryzen 7 (8 cores, 16 threads)
- **RAM**: 32GB DDR4
- **GPU**: NVIDIA RTX 3070 or equivalent with CUDA support
- **Storage**: 1TB SSD
- **OS**: Ubuntu 22.04 LTS or Windows 10/11 with WSL2
- **Network**: Gigabit Ethernet for multi-robot simulations

## Recommended Specifications

For optimal performance, especially with Isaac Sim and complex simulations:

- **CPU**: Intel i9 or AMD Ryzen 9 (12+ cores, 24+ threads)
- **RAM**: 64GB DDR4
- **GPU**: NVIDIA RTX 4080 or higher for Isaac Sim
- **Storage**: 2TB NVMe SSD
- **Network**: Gigabit Ethernet for multi-robot simulations

## Specialized Hardware

While not required for learning the concepts, the following hardware can enhance your experience:

- **Physical Humanoid Robot**: Optional but recommended for real-world validation
- **LiDAR Sensors**: For real-world validation of perception algorithms
- **RGB-D Cameras**: For perception tasks and computer vision
- **IMU Sensors**: For motion tracking and control validation

## Additional Considerations

- **Cooling**: High-performance cooling system recommended for sustained computation
- **Power Supply**: Adequate power supply for high-end GPU
- **Monitor**: Multiple monitors recommended for development workflow

## Budget Considerations

- **Minimum Setup**: ~$2,500-3,500 USD
- **Recommended Setup**: ~$4,000-6,000 USD
- **Professional Setup**: ~$6,000+ USD

## Compatibility Notes

- Isaac Sim requires NVIDIA RTX series GPUs for optimal performance
- Some advanced features may require specific hardware configurations
- Always check compatibility with your chosen robotics platform
